Mac and CHeese

What kinda mac and cheese we talkin about?

These are perhaps the most bomb ass fantastic mac and cheese you will ever eat, and I'm going to tell you how. Its a simple recipe too, and you can make it any time

Ingredients

  • Your favourite mac and cheese box
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Your toppings of choice. Mine are:
    • hot dogs
    • hamburger helper

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni to your liking (I like al dente)
  2. Drain the water from the macaroni, but keep them in the pot
  3. Add 1 tbsp of butter, 2-3 tbsp of milk and the seasoning packet
  4. Mix, and add your toppings
