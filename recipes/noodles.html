Noodles

What kinda noodles we talkin about?

These are perhaps the most bomb ass fantastic noodles you will ever eat, and I'm going to tell you how. Its a simple recipe too, and you can make it any time

Ingredients

  • Your favourite instant noodles (I prefer nong shim)
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Your toppings of choice. Mine are:
    • Seaweed
    • Crab sticks
    • Some kind of pork

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles to your liking (I like al dente)
  2. Drain the water from the noodles, but keep them in the pot
  3. Add 1 tbsp of butter, 2-3 tbsp of milk and about 1/4 of the seasoning packet
  4. Mix, and add your toppings
